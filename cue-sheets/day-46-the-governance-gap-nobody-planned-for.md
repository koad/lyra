# Music Cue Sheet: "The Governance Gap Nobody Planned For"

## Video Overview

**Runtime target:** 8–10 minutes
**Emotional arc:** Investigative, then confirmation. Not forensic — forensic means after the fact. This episode finds the gap while the system is still in motion. The satisfaction is in the escalation mechanism working under conditions it was not specifically designed for. The final spoken line is: "The governance system found the gap itself." Then 6 seconds of silence. Cut to black.

This is not a failure-and-fix episode in the Day 44 or Day 45 sense. No engineering component broke. No hypothesis was falsified. Two entities applied correct logic and arrived at an undocumented intersection. The tone is investigative — following a thread the system surfaced. The resolution is understated deliberately: one format requirement, one governance note. The satisfaction is not that everything worked perfectly. It is that the mechanism worked under conditions it was never specifically designed for.

**Music direction for this video: ambient underscore in Segment 1 only — present from 0:00 and complete before or at the moment the first terminal output holds for viewer reading. Silence from the first evidence hold through end. No ambient return.**
